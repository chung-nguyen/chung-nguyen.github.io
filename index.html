Our company

Our advantage

As a highland with lush green environment, Gia Lai is enrich with wood industry. Instead of wasting chip wood from other factories, we turn it into wood pellets. We simply has the raw materials right at our door, and we help preventing them from being wasted.

Our commitment

As residents of the province, we are obliged to respect and protect our natural landscape. We are committed to sustainable, renewable harvesting techniques that ensure our forests are as beautiful and vibrant tomorrow as they are today.

As you probably know, wood pellets reduce usage of oil, and are one of the cleanest energy sources for home and industrial heating. We don’t use cheap fillers and additives, so our pellets are a carbon-neutral heating choice that safeguards our air.
